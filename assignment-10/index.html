The Era of the Flower Child

The 70s were an era in which bell bottoms and free love reigned supreme,
Jimi Hendrix, Joe Cocker and Jerry Garcia sang sweet ballads and slapped
basses or something. I picture veganism and body odor masked with patchouli,
Hippies selling tabs of acid for a dollar in Golden Gate
park, wearing black armbands and no shirt. Janice Joplin was an incredible yeller.
