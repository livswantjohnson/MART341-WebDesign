Cucumber Caprese Salad

So good I didn't get a picture before it was gone!

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Basil
  • Mozzerella
  • Garbonzo Beans
  • Balsamic Vineagar
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Chop cucumber, tomato, basil and mozzerella into bite size pieces
  2. Open the can of garbanzo beans
  3. Drain and Rinse the beans. Phoebe says beans only make you fart if you don't rinse them
  4. Mix the salad ingredients in a large bowl
  5. Dowse in balsamic vinegar and salt to taste
  6. Enjoy!
